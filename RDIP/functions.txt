Functionalities of experiment page:

1)We have an option called "Select language" which has two options, English and Hindi.
2)On selecting a language, we get words from a sentence as buttons which are random in order.
3)As we start selecting the buttons, a button named "Re-form the sentence" will appear and the content of the selected buttons will be printed above the "Re-form" button.
4)After we select all the buttons, "Check correctness of the sentence" button will be enabled.
5)If "Check" button is clicked, it will validate the sentence and return whether it is Right or Wrong Answer.
6)If Wrong answer is displayed, another button "Get correct sentence" will be displayed, showing the correct sentences from the random words.
